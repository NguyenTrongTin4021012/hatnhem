Project Concept

Specialty Coffee for the People

Authentic Problem

Specialty coffee is technical and intimidating (Hạt). Hidden roasteries are hard to find (Hẻm). We remove the "Bad Order" risk by matching DNA to roast profiles.

The MVP Strategy

A high-performance web app using Gemini AI as a Logic Engine. It translates simple cravings into technical roasting metrics.

Visual Language

Using Chart.js to visualize the "Coffee DNA" makes technical data accessible. The Leaflet map provides a curated journey through HCMC.

Technical Moat

Deterministic local scoring ensures the app works even if the AI fails. The data-driven pairing logic is proprietary and district-specific.

Project Concept

Bridging the Hạt & Hẻm

The "Hạt" (The Bean)

Specialty coffee is often seen as technical and intimidating. We translate TDS, roast levels, and extraction methods into a visual DNA profile that anyone can understand.

The "Hẻm" (The Alley)

Saigon's best coffee is hidden in alleys. Our map doesn't just show locations; it captures the "Alley Soul" — the vibe, the energy, and the sense of place.

The Problem

Discovery tools like Google Maps focus on ratings, but not palate-match. Consumers risk expensive orders they don't enjoy, while roasteries struggle to find their ideal audience.

The Tech Stack

Developed by Nguyen Trong Tin for RMIT Digital Media Studio 4. Built using HTML5, Leaflet.js, Chart.js, and Google Gemini AI for deterministic tasting logic.
